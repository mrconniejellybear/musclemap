      Why Does This Work?

      Descriptions withblue text denote a hyperlink to a supporting source

      I am a research assistant at WWU earning my masters in experimental psychology. Over the past year, I have been building this site as an accessible, user-friendly resource for high-quality information about the body. I have designed this website with my dear friend Zoe Rust.

      Strong skeletal muscles are important to maintain quality of life, and exercise starts with understanding of the body. This project is for anyone looking to round-out their muscle knowledge, in or out of the gym.

      Feel free to leave feedback to help me improve the site — Thank you for using MuscleMap!
