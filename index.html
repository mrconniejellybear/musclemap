      I am an undergrad research assistant earning my masters in experimental psychology. Over the past year, I have been building this site to offer an accessible, user-friendly resource for high-quality information about the body.
      Strong skeletal muscles are important to maintain quality of life, and exercise starts with understanding of the body. This project is for anyone looking to round-out their muscle knowledge, in or out of the gym.
