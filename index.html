    Why Does This Work?

      I am an undergraduate research assistant at Western Washington University studying behavioral neuroscience. When I started this site, there was no existing index of the muscular system. While textbooks and websites offered provided a necessary foundation for MuscleMap, a 3D model offers a more hands-on, interactive learning opportunity. High-quality information—especially information about the human body body—should never be used for profit. Information should be free and accessible for everyone.

      Western Washington University has supported this project by hosting it as a part of their kinesology program. The images, models, and exercise claims featured on this site have been approved by Professor Jun G. San Juan, PhD, ATC, and are additionally supported by external sources. The ecorche was modeled and licensed by Alex Lashko.

      In the future, I plan to expand this site to other areas of the body, such as the brain and skeleton. For now, given that I am developing and hosting this website independently, any feedback, positive or negative, that helps me improve the site is welcome and appreciated; My links can be found below. — Thank you for giving your time to my work!
