      Using the Viewer
      Hover over the model to preview muscle names. Double-click to select, single-click to deselect (hand icon).
      Using the cursor, click and drag to rotate the model 360°. Alternatively, use the orbit controls at the bottom of the viewer.
      Zoom using your trackpad, scroll wheel, or the native zoom controls.
      While a muscle is selected, click the puzzle piece icon to isolate it from the rest of the model.
      Click the contrast icon to swap between a high-contrast and striated model.
      To re-center the model, click the target icon.
      I am a research assistant at WWU earning my masters in experimental psychology. Over the past year, I have been building this site as an accessible, user-friendly resource for high-quality information about the body.
      Strong skeletal muscles are important to maintain quality of life, and exercise starts with understanding of the body. This project is for anyone looking to round-out their muscle knowledge, in or out of the gym.
